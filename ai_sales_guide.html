The Framework

Building the Pizza

Every client starts with a Sauce — their core need. Then they pick Toppings to customize.

🍕 Think of it like building a pizza.

The dough is AI — it's what makes everything possible. The sauce is the core function the client needs most. The toppings are individual features they choose to add on. Nobody orders the same pizza. Some want just cheese. Some want everything. Your job is to find out what they're hungry for.

The Process

Sales Flow

Follow this on every conversation.

1

Find the Pain Point

Ask questions until you hear frustration. Don't pitch anything until you've identified the specific problem costing them time or money.

Missed calls? No-shows? Slow follow-up? Unpaid invoices? Staff overload?
2

Match the Solution

Don't walk through every feature. Only explain the problem, the outcome, and the ROI. One clear solution to one clear problem.

Name the problem Show the outcome State the ROI
3

Add Simple Upsells

Once they've committed to one solution, offer one natural add-on. Frame it as a question, not a feature.

"Would it also help if…" "One thing clients add…"
